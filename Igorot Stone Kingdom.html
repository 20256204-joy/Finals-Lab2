  • Igorot Stone Kingdom Map

    • Situated along Long-Long Road in Pinsao Proper Village, the Igorot Stone Kingdom will surely capture your attention with beautiful layers of stone-formed castle structure. The Stone Kingdom, with an area of 6,000 square meters surrounded by tall trees and created resembling rice terraces, was built by Pio Velasco. From a birds-eye view, the place looks like a ruin of an old Roman Empire castle.
